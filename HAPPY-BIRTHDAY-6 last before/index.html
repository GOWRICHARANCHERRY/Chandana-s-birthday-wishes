Some words for you

To my favourite person — I just want to say a few things to you. Today you are in my life, and I wish that every year to come, you will always be by my side. No matter how much I say about you, it will always be less. But I want to say this — the girl I first spoke to in 2023 (yes, I am talking about you, don't think otherwise)… and today, this girl has the whole sky and earth within her. She has developed this within herself just because she loves me a lot — and I know that. But did you love me from the start? No… because you were scared of people and society, the ones who use girls day by day and look down on them. After knowing all this, any girl would be afraid to get into a relationship — especially girls from good families, which is what you are. But you know what, Chandhu? When you hold the right person's hand, the world becomes this happy. You will have a confidence inside you that there is someone who loves me even more than I love myself. And I am always thankful… that after being rejected 4 or 5 times, you finally accepted me. That means "you believed in yourself that you could trust this boy." One of the best moments of my life is when you chose me… when you had the best options available, and after all of that, you chose me. That's no small thing. The big thing is — even after knowing everything about the past, you stayed with me. Just tell me one thing — even after knowing how I am, I can never imagine letting you go, no matter how angry I get. I don't just say it casually — "I am the very lucky person to have you." I understood your importance long ago, so no matter how big the problem is, I will bow my head in front of you and listen to everything. Because… if someone else was in your place, I would be single today, and I wouldn't be this excited about a birthday, wouldn't be this happy. Your wishes, your love, your care, your affection — even your anger and your fierce side — I love all of it. Because the mentality you have is something not even 10 other people have. I don't know if I have said too much… but I truly love you from the bottom of my heart. Of course, there will always be something about you that I will keep discovering — and that is how it should be. "Because you taught me how important joy is in life, and how to share it with your own people. How to respect your person, how to help them grow, how to support them… I am still learning so much from you. So hats off to you, Chandhu." Never forget — you are also the best person in someone's life (my life, that is). So never make yourself small, never bring yourself down. Hold your head high and live. Live with happiness in your heart. Life is only one — enjoy all of it. Never be afraid…
Your love, gowri charan ❤️
